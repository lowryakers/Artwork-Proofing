Tips for best results
  • Packaging type controls eyemark: select Stick Pack / Sachet to enable the eyemark contrast check. Pouches and rigid packaging skip it automatically.
  • Text-as-outlines PDFs (standard for print) will have limited OCR accuracy. Results are best-effort with "verify manually" guidance where OCR is uncertain.
  • One PDF per SKU gives the clearest per-SKU report.
  • GTIN list is required for Brand Proof — it must include gtin and flavor / product name columns.
  • FDA flags are a starting point, not legal advice. Always have a regulatory professional sign off on final labels.
